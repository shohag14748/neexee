Neexee is the Mailvolution
(not just another email client).

No Email Killer is not going to make email disappear. But email is a solution from the 70s that has barely improved lately. Major changes have been to add bold and italics!.

We’ve been working for years to detect email weaknesses and flaws, and we’ve come up with new ideas, improvements and solutions. We’ve built from the ground up a revolutionary tool to make email useful and productive. At last.

Inline answers, automatic follow-up reminders, real-time information on who’s answering the mail, read status, A.I powered dashboard, #tags, @mentions, >tasks and +deadlines within the body, a version control system for attachment, and so on.

A state-of-the-art tool that converts emails into collaborative conversations, correctly organized and presented. One single tool that allows real-time chatting with the participants and including the session as part of the email. A tool to automatically remind you of your pending mails, and of the ones that you’re waiting for an answer. One tool that takes business communications (both internal and external) to a new level
